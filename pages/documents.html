List of Documents to be submitted by candidates seeking admission through Institute Level Rounds

Sequence of documents
  1. DTE Acknowledgement
  2. MHT-CET 2021/ JEE(Main) Score Card.
  3. Statement of HSC (Std.XII)/Diploma mark sheet.
  4. Statement of Marks obtained at S.S.C./X or its equivalent qualifying examination
  5. Leaving certificate /Transfer Certificate
  6. Domicile/Birth Certificate
  7. Migration Certificate(for Other than HSC board candidates)
  8. Aadhaar Card
  9. Nationality Certificate

Note:   Three sets of photocopies (not attested) of all documents applicable for the respective candidate.
